Privacy Policy

All Aboard ATS Auto-Filler Chrome Extension

Last updated: January 2026

This privacy policy explains how the All Aboard ATS Auto-Filler Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles your information.

Summary: Your information is stored locally in your browser. We do not collect, sell, or transmit your data to our servers. Data is used only within the Extension to assist with filling job application forms on third-party websites you choose.

Information Stored

The Extension may store information you choose to provide, including but not limited to:

  • First and last name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Home address and previous addresses
  • Employment history (employers, roles, dates)
  • Education history (schools, degrees, GPA)
  • References and related contact information

This information is used solely to assist with filling out job application forms.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All information is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's extension storage.

The Extension does not store your data on external servers owned or operated by us.

Data Collection & Sharing

We do not:

  • Collect your data on our servers
  • Sell or rent your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use analytics, tracking, or advertising tools

Your data is only used locally by the Extension and is submitted only to job application websites you choose to interact with.

User Control & Data Deletion

You are always in control of your data.

  • You can edit or delete stored information at any time within the Extension
  • You can remove all stored data by uninstalling the Extension
  • No data persists after removal of the Extension

Third-Party Websites

When you apply for a job, your information is submitted directly to third-party websites (such as employer career pages or applicant tracking systems). Their handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policies, not this one.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page and within the Extension.
